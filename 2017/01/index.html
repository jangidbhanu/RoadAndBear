By tushar singh - January 25, 2017
January 25, 2017




Kodaikanallies at an altitude of 7000 feet. It is the perfect getaway if you are exhausted from fighting the harsh heat of Tamil Nadu. Famous for its alluring sights and pleasing weather, it is known as "Princess of hill stations". Kodai also has a magical effect on your senses, stoners this is for you.


GETTING THERE :
Kodai is well connected to all major cities. Numerous buses and taxis can take you up there. I personally took a bus from Chennai to Kodai which is about a 10 hour journey and will cost you around 500rs. There also many trains available . Kodai is round and about  530 kms from Chennai and 120 km from Madurai.


STAYING THERE:

There are many options from small cozy cottages to small motels to luxurious hotels. Cottages cost about 2,000 to 3,000rs and provides a homely stay. Hotels range from 500 to 4,000rs per night.  Since travelling with friends , we resided in a small cottage and it really was the way to enjoy Kodai.

By tushar singh - January 23, 2017
The gateway to 6 of the 8 tallest peak on this planet , including Mount Everest (Sagarmatha), Nepal is known for it's mesmerizing views of the great Himalayas. It is home to one of the most famous spiritual leader, Gautam Buddha and will help you attain self enlightenment. Nepal will give you a every second adventure because you can never be sure what lies ahead of you.
The beauty of the Pokhara valley, Kathmandu and Sagarmatha will capture you in its chain.




GETTING THERE:
Traveling to Nepal is quite a easy journey. You can board a flight to Kathmandu which will cost you around rs. 5000/- one way. I personally saved all I could by taking a train form Delhi to Gorakhpur. Buses shuttle across between Sunauli(Nepal) and Gorakhpur and take about 3 hours. From there Kathmandu is just a overnight trip to start you journey to the unknown.

By Anurag Singh - January 22, 2017
One of the best travel destination in India. Kerala known as the "God's Own Country". It attracts a lot of tourists from all over the world every year. Resting between the western ghat and the Arabian sea. Kerala is the most wild and trippy state to travel. On the East stands the marvelous Munnar covered by lavish tea plantations. The nature calling Meesapulimala along with the costal areas of Kerala will carry you away for the stressful urban daily life. Alleppey is best known for houseboat cruises along the rustic Kerala backwaters.

GETTING THERE:
Kerala has a well connected railway and state run buses for cheap and easy traveling. I took a overnight train from Chennai to Thiruvananthapuram.

One of the most developed coastal cities of India, Trivandrum will give you a mind boggling luxurious experience with a range of 5 Stars and small affordable hotels at your footsteps.  Trivandrum is a great place to start your journey.
