By tushar singh - January 25, 2017
January 25, 2017





Kodaikanal lies at an altitude of 7000 feet. It is the perfect getaway if you are exhausted from fighting the harsh heat of Tamil Nadu. Famous for its alluring sights and pleasing weather, it is known as "Princess of hill stations". Kodai also has a magical effect on your senses, stoners this is for you.


GETTING THERE :

Kodai is well connected to all major cities. Numerous buses and taxis can take you up there. I personally took a bus from Chennai to Kodai which is about a 10 hour journey and will cost you around 500rs. There also many trains available . Kodai is round and about  530 kms from Chennai and 120 km from Madurai.
STAYING THERE:
There are many options from small cozy cottages to small motels to luxurious hotels. Cottages cost about 2,000 to 3,000rs and provides a homely stay. Hotels range from 500 to 4,000rs per night.  Since travelling with friends , we resided in a small cottage and it really was the way to enjoy Kodai.



PLACES TO VISIT:
1.     Vattakanal



                                                                                     
  1.  Kodai lake : located at the center of the station, this cold  lake provides us pleasant view surrounded by the  mountains. It is a great place to spend your day enjoying  the nearby street food. You may also decide to go  boating.  

                                                                                           
  1.   Coakers Walk: A gratifying walk to appreciate the beauty   of the plains from the top of the trek.  
  


                                                                       
1.      Dolphin Nose: A smooth trek of a few kilometers to stand at the tip of the Dolphins nose to race your heart beat.(Actually, we did a trek to this point and once we reached there one caretaker of that point asked us like from where you all are coming ,we told him the hotel name and he replied very curiously that you came here all the long doing 18kms of trek! and we were like "yeah ,this is an achievement!")                        

                                                                                                           
 
3.    Pillar Rock: Though the most beautiful sight it is densely packed with footsteps.

4.    Pine Forest:  Prefect for a solo picture. You can also have   fun by riding a horse.



TIME TO VISIT: 

Traveling in winters will let you feel the real coolness of Kodai with the warm feelings of bonfire and hot street food such as idlis , wadas and maggie.
In Summers although relatively hot that the winter season it is still much better than some of its counter parts. 

BACK TO PAVILION:

Although there is a bus every hour to Chennai due to the wonderful phenomena to pre-booking we weren't able to get a bus. Hence we caught a bus to Madurai which gave us the chance to visit the renowned Meenaakshi Temple. From there we traveled back to Chennai.
